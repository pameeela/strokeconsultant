Welcome to StrokeConsultant.com.

Starting on Sept. 1, 2010, Jamie Barone will be opening a Michael Phelps Swim School at the Greenwich Catholic pool. Click here to register.

Jamie joined the full-time coaching staff of the North Baltimore Aquatic Club on Sept. 1, 2009. He is the head coach of the 13-14 year old age group swimmers.

Although he is coaching in Baltimore seven days per week, Jamie returns to Fairfield County occasionally to work with young swimmers. Olympian Adam Sioui, who represented Canada at the Beijing Olympic Games, continues to coach privately in Greenwich and Stamford. Contact us to set up a lesson with Jamie or Adam at fastlaneswimming@gmail.com.

Shoulder pain? It's almost always about technique. Physicians are referring swimmers with shoulder pain to Jamie. We usually can identify the problem and correct it within the first session.

We continue to offer clinics for triathletes. The weakest part of most triathletes' background is in swimming. Using our underwater camera, innovative coaching techniques and personalized instruction methods, Stroke Consultant can improve a triathlete's efficiency in the water.

WHAT JAMIE CAN DO FOR YOU

  • Analyze your strokes, turns and starts and help correct flaws in technique.
  • Provide personalized instruction in all aspects of swimming, including developing a positive attitude.
  • For example: In 37 swims at the final meet with Jamie as the head coach, the Greenwich High School Girls' Junior Varsity Team swam 34 personal best times.
